School Achievements

BAASC swimming Competition
first place in both the under 9 and under 11 categories.

The Primary School BAASC swim meet was held on the 20th of April at Stonehill International School The competing schools were TRIO World Academy, Canadian International School, and Bangalore International School. The competition ended with Stonehill winning first place in both the under-9 and under-11 categories. Congratulations to our Tigers!

Inter School Football Competition
Stonehill Tigers win the BAASC U-15 Middle School Football Championships.

Stonehill Tigers won the BAASC U-15 Middle School Football Championships held at Canadian International School on the 25th of April Stonehill won against five competing schools and scored 3-1 in the final match against Legacy School. Go Tigers!

Horse Riding Competition
Jaiveer, Grade 8, wins Second Place!

Jaiveer, grade 8, participated in the Equestrian Premier League, held at Embassy International Riding School (EIRS), Bangalore. Jaiveer placed second in the overall best rider, under 14, category. Congratulations Jaiveer!

What's New

  • New School Library

    With our growing enrollment, more classroom spaces are needed hence the old libraries are being converted into Student Support Services and EAL offices, classroom spaces, and a Secondary Art studio.


  • New football team

    The Stonehill Soccer 7s is scheduled for December 9th, 2023, marking the debut of this tournament in Bangalore. About twenty teams from various international schools will participate, offering students a chance to display their football skills in a competitive and supportive environment. The event aims to create a platform for impactful change while promoting student talent in football.
